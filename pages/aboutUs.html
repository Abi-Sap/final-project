ABOUT US

1999: Our Journey Begins

Our story is one of the resilience and truimph that began as an idea. Dropout and tennage pregnancies were rampant in our community, teens engaged in alot in activites because of financial constraint. Aside that the communities lacked structures and facilities to make live simple and easier. These people as the target, we decided to build an NGO that will cater for these people.

The journey continues

These desires brought alot of questions; how to fund these project was the big question. Creating businesses was the only rescue. The first was a farm project. Growing annual cropd then later venture into Biennial and Perennial crops. The market expanded, lots of youths were employed whiles ensuring safety cosumption. Investors came in and we entered into other businesses such as animal rearing, rubber plantation, production of plastics. e.t.c.
