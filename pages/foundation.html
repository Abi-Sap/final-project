BXEPT FOUNDATION

Bxept Foundation was set up in 1999 to support the work of NGO's seeking to relieve poverty, ensure safe sanitation and healthy communities, advocate for quality education, involved in environmental sustainability and also talks about economic development.

Bxept also support individuals who needs support to learn a skill to improve their lives. We also support individuals who need financial assistance in education, enteprenuership e.t.c.

We hold conferences, bussiness seminars, enteprenuership conferences and many other. We currently operate in 10 regions and working towards reaching the other regions and beyond the nation.
