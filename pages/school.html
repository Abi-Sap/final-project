Be An Exception International School


About

Be An Exception International School, founded in 2005, is a renowned educational institution dedicated to providing a holistic learning experience to students from diverse backgrounds. The school has campuses in all the 16 regions in Ghana, our school boasts a rich history of academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement.



History

The School was established with a vision to foster a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Since its inception, the school has been committed to offering high-quality education that prepares students for success in the ever-evolving global landscape.


Over the years, the School has evolved and adapted to meet the changing needs of students and society. From its humble beginnings with a small group of dedicated educators, the school has grown into a vibrant learning community serving hundreds of students each year.


Throughout its history, the School has upheld a tradition of academic rigor, promoting critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning. Our innovative curriculum, state-of-the-art facilities, and dedicated faculty members ensure that students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for the challenges of the 21st century.


Beyond academics, the School is deeply committed to fostering a culture of inclusivity, respect, and community service. Through various extracurricular activities, service projects, and partnerships with local organizations, students are encouraged to become compassionate leaders and active contributors to society.


As the School continues to grow and evolve, we remain steadfast in our commitment to excellence, equity, and student success. With a proud history and a bright future ahead, we invite you to join us on our journey of learning, discovery, and growth at the School.

SHS



B.A.E Senior High School (SHS) stands as a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development in our community. Its rich history is a testament to the tireless efforts of visionary educators and passionate stakeholders who sought to redefine secondary education. The inception of BAE SHS was a response to the growing demand for a more comprehensive and specialized learning environment. In the early years, we shared the same vision with a dedicated group of educators and community leaders. We want to would empower students to reach their full potential, academically, socially, and emotionally. From its humble beginnings in a modest building, BAE SHS quickly gained recognition for its innovative approach to education. The school's curriculum was carefully designed to provide students with a well-rounded learning experience, blending academic rigor with practical skills and real-world applications.
